OUR FOUNDER, A TRUE LEADER, ALANAH RODRIGUEZ

Alanah Rodriguez is best know saying "Nobody leaves here without learning something new, everyday." Founder and Chief Executive Officer of Clear Glass, Rodriguez is a hands-on leader who continually helps to perfect Clear Glass' products, improving transaction friction points, and providing the best rates for clients. Rodriguez believes that by tracking and monitoring the right data, you can improve on anything in business. "Having a team of 105 professionals also helps," she often adds.

Clear Glass improved scratch resistance by 60% in the last two years. They've also cut credit rebills in half and switched to a primarily eletronic accounting system. She is proudest of how the quality accounting she's responsible for complement the quality of Clear Glass' products.
